Topic Model Overview

This project explores political news bias by analyzing article text from major outlets. A topic model (LDA) was used to uncover the most common clusters of themes in the dataset. Each topic represents a group of words that tend to appear together across multiple articles.

Identified Topics

  • Topic 1: Government, elections, policy, party, president
  • Topic 2: Economy, inflation, jobs, taxes, business
  • Topic 3: Climate, environment, science, renewable energy
  • Topic 4: Crime, justice, law enforcement, safety
  • Topic 5: Health, pandemic, medicine, public safety

These discovered topics highlight the thematic differences between networks. For instance, CNN tends to emphasize global issues and scientific reporting, while Fox News more often emphasizes political leadership and economic framing.
